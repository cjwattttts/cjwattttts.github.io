Retail Performance & Trend Analysis

Western US Sales Trend Dashboard

In this project, I analyzed retail sales trends in the Western US, focusing on identifying key outliers and trends that could inform business decisions. Using Excel and Tableau, I created an interactive dashboard that allowed users to drill down into specific regions and sales categories, visualizing performance data in a dynamic way. The dashboard provided insights into regional variations, seasonal fluctuations, and outlier performances, ultimately helping stakeholders identify areas for improvement and growth.

Tools Used: Excel, Tableau
