Western US Sales Trend Dashboard

In this project, I explored a large dataset and created visuals to uncover trends and insights. I began by developing bar graphs to show profit levels for various products across regions in 2022 and 2023. Next, I created a tree map for each item and category, where the size of each block represented sales volume, with the largest product, Lagan, generating $195,034,677 in sales. The project also includes interactive filters for users to customize data by year, category, and item. A detailed chart visualizes sales amounts for items in the West region, while a profit map shows state-level profits in the Western US using color gradients, red for high profits and blue for low. A key feature is the interactive dashboard, allowing users to drill down into data for specific items in the West and view state-level performance. Users can click on a state to see more detailed product data.
