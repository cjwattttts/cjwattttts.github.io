Southern US Sales Performance

This project analyzes sales and profit trends across the Southern U.S., revealing major fluctuations in machine sales from 2020 to 2023. I began by cleaning and structuring the dataset in Excel to ensure accuracy before importing it into Tableau for visualization. The analysis highlights how machine sales peaked at $27,594 in 2020 but dropped sharply to $3,113 in 2021, with continued instability in the following years. The findings uncover North Carolina's machine sales in 2023 as a key loss driver, with Burlington alone losing nearly $1,000 in profit.

 

Tools Used: Tableau, Excel

 

Formulas, Skills, and Techniques Used: Bar Graphs, Filters, Profit Map, Color Mark, Label Mark, Detail Mark, Horizontal Bar Graph, Interactive Dashboard, Story, Story Text Boxes

Interactive dashboard showing sales trends by year and product category across the Southern U.S.
Story interface guiding viewers through a narrative of profit loss and machine sales instability.
