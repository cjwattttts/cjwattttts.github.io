Risk Management Dashboard

In this project, I developed a Business Intelligence dashboard that tracks and visualizes risk levels across various departments. The dashboard allows users to interact with data in real-time, filtering and drilling down to view specific risk metrics and trends. It was designed to help stakeholders identify potential risks, track their mitigation efforts, and manage risks effectively across different teams.

 

Tools Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

 

Formulas, Skills, and Techniques Used: DOM Manipulation, createElement, appendChild, removeChild, event listeners, stopPropagation(), dynamic risk card creation, form handling, background color styling based on data, CSS Flexbox for layout, JavaScript functions for adding and removing risk items, handling button clicks for bulk updates, categorizing risk levels, and handling event propagation in complex user interactions.
